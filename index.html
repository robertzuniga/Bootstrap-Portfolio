About Me

If you're seeking a Full Stack Developer, keep reading....

I am currently enrolled in the Coding Boot Camp at UNC Charlotte.

The Coding Boot Camp is developed by Trilogy Education Services and taught through UNC Charlote Extension.

The curriculum includes front-end and back-end technologies through a dynamic full-stack curriculum, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, React.js, Node.js, C#, .Net, Database Theory, Bookshelf.js, MongoDB, MySQL, Command Line, as well as Git.

An important feature of the Coding Boot Camp is that we work together with our peers to tackle complex projects and get hands-on training through experiential learning opportunities with employers.

The highlight of the Coding Boot Camp is that we take our new skills to the next level on challenging problems, building a portfolio of apps and projects to showcase our knowledge.
